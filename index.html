Generic Blog Design

This is my first submission to The Open Design Community. Feel free to use it however you like. The only thing I ask is that you leave a link to my site http://summitr.com/ somewhere on your site. I don't really care where it is. You can add it in the footer, a links section on the sidebar, or on a links page. This Design is probably best used for a personal blog. I don't think the colors really lend themselves to a business site. I plan on porting this to Wordpress at sometime in the future. Oh yeah, this Design is Valid XHTML 1.0 Strict and CSS.
